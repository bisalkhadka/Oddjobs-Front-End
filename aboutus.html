ODDJOBS We are more than just a job site. From the web, to mobile, to social media tools and apps, we service companies of all sizes to find the right fit using most advanced technology in Nepal. Providing recruitment solutions to employers finding, fostering and preparing the right candidates in every possible ways with an effective tracking system and a dedicated team of customer service to both; the employers and the job seekers, has always been our primary goal.

If you want to be resourceful, you can earn extra income by helping clients with quick jobs. You can help people with tasks when they don't have the time, knowledge or skills to do tasks themselves. Since odd jobs are short-term, they can supplement a person's income while between jobs or bring in extra cash flow. Odd jobs add experience to a resume. Also, the contacts you make could lead to permanent employment.
